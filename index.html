Our Story

Beyond the Horizon

​The story of Aquila Tours and Safaris LLC began with a simple act of kindness. Our founder, Abdul Salam, started his career as a taxi driver, where his politeness and friendly nature were his greatest assets. One day, he met a traveler who was so impressed by his service that he suggested a partnership—Salam would handle the pickups and tours for his travel company. ​This experience opened Abdul Salam's eyes to a new passion. He realized that the same qualities that made him a great driver—a customer-first approach, a commitment to kindness, and a deep knowledge of the UAE—could be the foundation of a new kind of travel company. ​With 20 years of experience in the field, Aquila Tours and Safaris LLC was born. Today, we are proud to be the trusted B2B partner for corporate clients, travel agencies, and event planners across the UAE. We believe that good service is not just about logistics; it’s about a personal, customer-friendly approach that makes every client feel valued. Our motto, "Beyond the Horizon," is a promise to deliver service that exceeds expectations, every single time.
